Who We Are

TrainBook is a simple and beginner-friendly online train ticket reservation system built with Django. We aim to make train booking accessible to everyone with an easy-to-use interface.

Our Mission

To provide a hassle-free train booking experience for all users. Our system is designed with simplicity in mind.

What We Offer
  • ✅ Easy registration and login process
  • ✅ Simple train ticket booking system
  • ✅ Email notifications for all bookings
  • ✅ Dashboard to manage your bookings
  • ✅ Profile management with photo upload
